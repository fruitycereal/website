Yu Ji-min (Korean: 유지민; born April 11, 2000), known professionally as Karina, is a South Korean singer, rapper and dancer. She is the leader of the South Korean girl group Aespa, formed by SM Entertainment in November 2020. She is also a member of the supergroup Got the Beat, which debuted on January 3, 2022.

Karina was born and raised in Suwon, Gyeonggi-do, South Korea, to a family consisting of her parents and an older sister. She attended Hansol High School until she was discovered by an SM talent scout through social media. She then dropped out to focus on training and later obtained a GED certificate.
